Get Involved

Who can join

Junior and Senior year students at the Early College at Guilford are able to tutor with EducateUS. This is because underclassmen will be in class during the 3:30 to 4:30 tutoring timeframe. No previous experience or training is required. Tutors can join any time of the year and are able to attend as many or as few sessions as they are able. If you cannot drive yourself, see our transportation information to learn more about how we can help you.

Why should you join

EducateUS is an amazing community service opportunity, providing a consistent source of volunteer hours. Each tutor earns 1 hour of service for tutoring, with carpooling drivers earning an additional hour.

How to join

If you would like to join our group of wonderful tutors please email educateus.ecg@gmail.com or contact us here. We will respond quickly to further orient you into the program. We are always looking for more tutors ready to help local students through a positive and productive learning environment.
