SAT® Reading Section Overview

All questions in the reading section correspond to at least one passage. Some passages may also contain visuals or charts. There will also be one set of "paired passages." These passages are meant to conform to high school complexity levels.

The questions on the test fall into the categories below. Click on a category to learn more.

  • Information and Ideas
    • Reading Closely
    • Citing Textual Evidence
    • Determing Central Ideas and Themes
    • Summarizing
    • Understanding Relationships
    • Interpreting Words and Phrases in Context
  • Rhetoric
    • Word Choice
    • Text Structure
    • Point of View
    • Purpose
    • Arguments
  • Synthesis
    • Analyzing Multiple Texts
    • Analyzing Quantitative Information

They may also fall into subject or content areas, such as social studies or science.
