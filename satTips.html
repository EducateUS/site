SAT® Tips

Here, you will find our tips on approaching the test content. But first, a few general reminders:

  • Every question is worth the same amount of points, regardless of difficulty (if you get stuck, MOVE ON!)
  • There is no penalty for wrong answers, so you should always put an answer down
    • Pick your favorite letter and guess it when you have no idea. Otherwise, try to eliminate and guess from there.
  • If you have extra time, go back and finish the questions you skipped, then look over your other answers, but try not to overthink them
