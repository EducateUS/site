EducateUS

Tutoring SAT Prep
EducateUS is a registered 501 "c" nonprofit organization operating in Greensboro, North Carolina. Functioning as one of the Early College at Guilfords several clubs, high school students are able to share their knowledge with local middle schools. After taking a brief hiatus during the 2020-2021 school year due to the global pandemic, we have since returned to the classroom to tutor students stronger than before.
