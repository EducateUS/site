SAT® Math Tips

There’s a reason why most top colleges have a higher average score in math than in reading/writing — it is easier to improve on the math section than the reading section, since this section is way more predictable. Regular practice on this section is very helful.

Calculator Use

Know how to use your calculator effectively — take practice tests with the calculator you'll be using! Remember that you don't have to use a calculator for every calculator active question.
