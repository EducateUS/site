Calculator Use

Know how to use your calculator effectively — take practice tests with the calculator you'll be using! Remember that you don't have to use a calculator for every calculator active question.

Avoiding Careless Mistakes

Remember that the answer choices are geared towards answers you might get if you made a mistake. Make sure that you are actually answering the problem. For example, if a question asks you to solve for 2x, you might want to circle that so you don't accidentally solve for x.

Time Management

SAT® Math questions generally get harder as they go. Remember this, especially towards the end. The hardest multiple choice will be followed by an easier grid-in, so you may want to skip ahead and get those points if you get stuck.
