Signing up for tutoring sessions

It is important for all tutors to sign up ahead of each session to ensure that their hours are logged correctly. Otherwise, you may not get credit for hours youve earned. When you sign-up, please specify your transportation. Complete your sign up using the link below.

Need a ride?

We utilize a carpooling system in which tutors with an extra seat in their car are able to drive those without a ride to the tutoring site while earning an extra service hour. In order to drive or be driven by another student, this permission form needs to be filled out.
